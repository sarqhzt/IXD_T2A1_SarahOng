What is Gen AI?

Gen AI stands for Generative Artificial Intelligence. Gen AI are computer programs that use artificial intelligence to create new content. Gen AI can help with tasks such as Text and Image Generation, Music Composition, and many more!

How do you prompt Gen AI?

1. Use natural language
2. Be specific and iterate
3. Be concise and clear
4. Keep the conversation going

What are some risks with Gen AI?

1. Misinformation in text generation
2. Video deepfakes
3. Fake images
4. Audio deepfakes
* Always make sure to S.U.R.E framework to spot any misinformation!
